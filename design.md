# Landing Page Design Plan

## 1. Objectives
* Create a slick, clean, and professional user experience.
* Drive user engagement and maximize conversion rates.
* Ensure responsive design and fast load times across all devices.

## 2. Visual Identity
* **Color Palette:** 
    * Background: `#FFFFFF` (Clean White) for ample whitespace.
    * Text: `#1F2937` (Dark Slate) for high readability.
    * Accent/CTA: `#2563EB` (Vibrant Blue) to draw attention to interactive elements.
* **Typography:**
    * Headings: *Inter* or *Roboto* (Clean, modern sans-serif).
    * Body text: *Open Sans* (Legible and neutral).

## 3. Layout & Structure
* **Header:** Sticky navigation bar featuring a minimalist logo (left), essential links (center), and a prominent CTA button (right).
* **Hero Section:** Large, bold headline clearly stating the value proposition, a brief sub-headline, a primary CTA button, and a high-quality product mockup or abstract vector graphic.
* **Features Section:** A 3-column grid layout utilizing minimalist SVG icons, short titles, and concise descriptions.
* **Social Proof:** A horizontal layout or carousel of clean testimonial cards (avatar, name, short quote).
* **Bottom CTA:** Full-width banner summarizing the primary offering accompanied by a final CTA button.
* **Footer:** Minimalist footer with copyright info, secondary navigation links, and subtle social media icons.

## 4. Recommended Tech Stack
* **Framework:** Next.js or React
* **Styling:** Tailwind CSS for rapid, clean, and consistent UI execution
* **Assets:** Heroicons (for SVG icons) and Google Fonts

## 5. ChemSearch Specific Layout (Trello-Inspired)

To bring the ChemSearch landing page to life with a Trello-like feel, the design should emphasize productivity, visual organization, and approachability.

### Hero Section
* **Headline:** "Chemistry Tools, Organized and Interactive."
* **Sub-headline:** "From element lookup and solubility graphing to automated Lewis structures. ChemSearch brings all the chemical data you need into one intuitive platform."
* **Visual:** A Trello-style isometric or overlapping mockup showing three "cards": a Periodic Table element card, a Chart.js solubility curve curve, and a Lewis structure diagram.
* **CTAs:** Primary `#2563EB` button: "Start Searching". Secondary outlined button: "View Examples".

### Alternating Feature Blocks (Trello-Style)
Instead of a simple 3-column grid, use alternating full-width bands with a 50/50 split of text and large UI visuals.
* **Block 1 (Text Left, Image Right):** "Instant Formula Analysis" — Highlight the lookup feature and molar mass calculations.
* **Block 2 (Image Left, Text Right):** "Automated Lewis Structures" — Show how typing *H2O* instantly builds a detailed bond and formal charge diagram.
* **Block 3 (Text Left, Image Right):** "Dynamic Solubility Graphing" — Emphasize the ability to compare multiple compounds on a single interactive temperature chart.

### Educational "Social Proof" & Trust
* **Section:** "Designed for Students and Educators"
* **Content:** Clean cards highlighting "No ads", "Instant accurate calculations", and "Visual learning". 

### Bottom Call to Action
* **Background:** A gentle wave or slanted divider transitioning to a vibrant `#2563EB` background.
* **Text:** "Ready to ace your chemistry homework?"
* **Button:** White background with blue text "Launch ChemSearch", hovering to an elevated shadow.
